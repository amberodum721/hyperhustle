⚠️ Sites to Avoid

The following types of sites and behaviors are known to be dangerous. Avoid them to protect yourself.

Ads

🚫 Warning Signs of Dangerous Sites

  • Multiple fake "Download" buttons — real button is usually small and specific
  • Sites asking you to disable your antivirus "to download"
  • Sites requiring you to complete surveys before downloading
  • Sites redirecting you through many pages before the actual download
  • Executable files (.exe) downloaded from random file hosts
  • Sites with excessive pop-ups that open new browser windows
  • Sites asking for your real name, phone number or credit card for "free" content
  • Unofficial "cracked" or "patched" versions of paid software

How to Protect Yourself

  • Install uBlock Origin — blocks most malicious ads and popups
  • Scan all downloaded files at VirusTotal.com before running them
  • Use ProtonVPN Free before torrenting
  • Use throwaway emails at Temp Mail when signing up for streaming sites
  • Keep your operating system and browser updated
  • Use a password manager like Bitwarden
